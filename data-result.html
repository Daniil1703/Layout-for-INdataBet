Opening and Closing odds lines

Data packages include pre-match open and close lines. For convenience, the date when this line was published by the bookmaker is indicated next to each coefficient.
The opening odds are the first pre-match odds published by the bookmaker for the event.
The closing odds are the last line before the start of the match that was published by the bookmaker for the event.
For visual convenience, the opening odds are green and the closing odds are red.
